FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How long are the video lessons?

Most lessons are between 30 seconds and 90 seconds. We believe in focused, high-quality micro-content over long lectures.

Which languages are available?

Currently English, Spanish, and French. More languages are added regularly as new creators join the platform.

Do I need any prior language experience?

Not at all. The feed surfaces content matched to your level, so beginners and advanced learners both find relevant lessons immediately.

How does the XP and streak system work?

You earn XP by completing quizzes. Correct answers give more XP. Your daily streak increases every day you complete at least one quiz. Miss a day and it resets.

Can I become a content creator?

Yes! Sign up and select the creator role during onboarding. You can upload videos and attach quizzes directly from your dashboard.
